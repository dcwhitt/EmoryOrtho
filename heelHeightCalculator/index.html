Upload a photo of both heels, calibrate the image using a coin or shoe-size estimate, then click the left and right heel points to estimate heel height difference.

How to take the photo

  1. Lie on your stomach with both knees just off the edge of a table, bed, or firm surface.
  2. Let both lower legs hang freely.
  3. Keep hips level and heels close together, but not touching.
  4. Place a coin flat on or near the heel if possible.
  5. Take the picture straight-on from the bottom of the feet, not from the side.
  6. Make sure both heels and the calibration object are visible.
Best accuracy: Use a coin for calibration. Shoe-size calibration is only an estimate and should be used when no coin is visible.

Measurement Setup

Shoe-size mode estimates foot length from shoe size. After selecting this mode, click heel and toe on one foot to calibrate.
